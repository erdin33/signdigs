Verify Signature

Enter a public key to verify a signer's authenticity. The system will check if the public key is associated with a registered user.

Enter the public key in hexadecimal format (usually found in the QR code)

How verification works:

  • DigiSign looks up the public key to verify the signer's identity
  • Public keys are unique cryptographic identifiers for each user
  • The verification confirms the key belongs to a registered user

Public keys should be copied exactly as they appear in the QR code or verification information.

Verification FAQ

Where can I find the public key?

The public key can be found in the QR code on signed documents, or it may have been shared with you directly by the signer.

What information will verification provide?

Verification will confirm if the public key belongs to a registered DigiSign user and provide their identity information including username and institution.

Can I still verify signed PDF documents?

Yes, you can also verify signed PDF documents by uploading them to our document verification system.
